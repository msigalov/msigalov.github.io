Hi, I'm Mikhail

I am a sophomore at Columbia University in the City of New York majoring in mechanical engineering and minoring in computer science. Design is my passion, and I am particularly interested in aerospace, robotics, and automotive engineering.

I have experience as a product designer, working in Solidworks and Creo Parametric to design mechanical subsystems for a diverse range of applications.

What I do

3D modelling, rapid manufacturing, prototyping, manufacturing, and assembly

Product Design

Rendering

Photography
