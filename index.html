Mikhail Sigalov
Engineering Student and Photographer

Hi, I'm Mikhail

I am a freshman at Columbia University majoring in Mechanical Engineering. Mechanical design is my passion, and I am interested in aerospace and robotics applications.

I am an experienced product designer, working in Solidworks and Creo Parametric. I am an alumni of the FIRST Robotics Competition program.

What I do

3D modelling and product design as well as mechanical and electrical system assembly.

Product Design

I create 3D models of parts in Solidworks or Creo Parametric, with the most experience in sheetmetal design and assembly modelling.

Photography

I'm an amateur photographer. I like searching for symmetry in the real world, and I enjoy capturing both city stills and people in action.

Rendering

I make high quality, realistic renders and animations of 3D computer models to help my clients visualize their final product.

Electronics Repair

I build desktops and have repaired desktops, laptops, and phones. I also have experience wiring and soldering.

Good design is finding that perfect balance between the way something looks and how it functions.
